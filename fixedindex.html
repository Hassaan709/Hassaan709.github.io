How to Play

Game Rules

Connect 4

  • Tap any column to drop your piece
  • First to connect 4 in a row wins
  • Winning pieces glow gold

Strategic Tic-Tac-Toe

  • Each player only has 3 pieces
  • After placing all 3, tap your piece then tap empty spot to move
  • Winning 3 glow gold

Dots & Boxes

  • Tap between dots to draw a line
  • Complete a box to claim it and get extra turn
  • Most boxes wins

Online Play

  • Host creates a room and shares the 6-digit code
  • Friend enters the room code to join
  • Host selects the game after both players connect
  • Game starts automatically for both players
